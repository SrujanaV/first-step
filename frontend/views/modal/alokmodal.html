Managing Editor of India’s number one business channel CNBC-Awaaz and India’s first Gujarati business channel CNBC-BAJAR, Alok is a journalist with an experience of around thirty years across print, television, radio and internet. Starting with Amrit Prabhat (Hindi daily of Amrit Bajar Patrika group), he worked with Navbharat Times and Dainik Jagran before joining the founding team of Aajtak in 1995. Staying with TV today network for almost six years, he was a part of the team that launched Aajtak (24 hour) news channel. While in Aajtak he produced and anchored shows like Business Aajtak and Subah Aajtak besides reporting extensively on business and politics. He worked with the BBC World service and worked on the launch of it’s website BBChindi.com. He was one of the main presenters of BBC Hindi’s flagship bulletin ‘aajkal’ when he decided to move back to India and join Network 18 to work on the launch of the hindi business channel that was to become CNBC-Awaaz. Before becoming Managing Editor, He was responsible for the market coverage and newsgathering operations of CNBC-Awaaz and overseeing the operations of Gujarati channel CNBC-BAJAR. He is one of the key founders of CNBC-Awaaz. Known for his sharp newssense, flare for writing and grasp on issues ranging from business and politics to art and literature. He is an avid reader and enjoys explaining complex issues in a lucid manner. He shares a strong bond and cordial relation within and outside the team due to his outreaching behavior and excellent people management skills.
